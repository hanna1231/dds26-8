In `ReserveStock()` and `ReleaseStock()` methods, change key construction:

IMPORTANT: The double braces `{{` and `}}` in f-strings produce literal `{` and `}` in the output. So `f"{{item:{request.item_id}}}"` produces `{item:abc123}` as the key — the hash tag is `{item:abc123}`.

The `self.db.get(item_key)` and `self.db.set(item_key, ...)` calls outside Lua are fine because RedisCluster routes individual commands to the correct node.
