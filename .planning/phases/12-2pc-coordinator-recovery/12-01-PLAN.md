Extend the transport layer (gRPC protos, servicers, client wrappers, queue consumers, queue client, transport adapter) with 2PC operations (prepare/commit/abort) for both Stock and Payment services.

Purpose: The 2PC coordinator (Plan 02) needs transport-agnostic prepare/commit/abort functions. This plan builds all 6 functions across both transport paths (gRPC and queue), so the coordinator can call them without knowing which transport is active.

Output: Updated protos with 2PC RPCs, regenerated pb2 files, extended gRPC servicers and queue consumers, new client/queue_client wrappers, updated transport adapter with all 6 new exports.
</objective>
